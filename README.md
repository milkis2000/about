This organization contains reverse-engineering and structural analysis archives of milkis's interactive game.

Uploaded repositories are intended for research, educational reference, preservation, and technical documentation purposes only.

The projects hosted here are not designed to provide playable or complete copies of the original games. Critical runtime assets such as textures, audio files, videos, proprietary resources, and other required content are intentionally omitted.

Most source structures remain partially or fully obfuscated as found in the original distributions. The repositories focus on studying architecture, behavior, rendering flow, asset loading, and gameplay systems rather than redistribution.

This organization does not endorse piracy, unauthorized redistribution, commercial misuse, or attempts to bypass ownership requirements of the original titles.

All rights to original games, assets, trademarks, and intellectual property belong to their respective owners.
